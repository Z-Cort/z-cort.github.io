GlobalSKU Show Booth

Designing the print materials for GlobalSKU's trade show booth.

Project Summary

"Make GlobalSKU impossible to miss on the show floor."

GlobalSKU was heading to a trade show and needed booth materials that could explain the product at a glance and pull people in from across the floor. I designed the tension fabric backdrop and a trifold brochure, then prepared everything for professional printing.

These materials needed to:

  • Communicate "turn photos into online listings" within seconds
  • Stay on-brand with GlobalSKU's seafoam gradient identity
  • Hold up at large-format print sizes with accurate color
  • Give visitors something to take home with pricing and features

Tools Used

Figma

Designing all of the booth materials and layouts.

Illustrator

Finalizing the print PDFs and converting to CMYK.

Photoshop

Touching up the product photos used throughout the materials.

Tension Fabric Backdrop

The backdrop is the first thing people see, so it leads with the core promise: "Turn Photos Into Online Listings." I paired the three-step feature callouts with real photos of the app in use, then ran them through Photoshop to clean them up before placing them. The marketplace logos along the bottom instantly answer "where can I sell?" without anyone needing to stop and ask.

Trifold Brochure

The trifold is the take-home piece. The outside cover mirrors the backdrop so visitors connect the two, while the panels cover key features and real social media quotes from users. Inside, a three-step "How It Works" walks through the app, backed by scannable item categories and the full subscription tier pricing. Everything was finalized in Illustrator and converted to CMYK for print.
