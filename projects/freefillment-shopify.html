FreeFillment Shopify App

Creating a brand logo, color guide, and promotional video in three days.

Project Summary

"Create a video that catches attention and stands out on the Shopify App Store — and a logo that fits the nature of the app."

FreeFillment needed a brand identity and a promotional video to launch on the Shopify App Store, all within three days. I was responsible for designing the logo, establishing a color guide, recording footage, and editing a finished promotional video from start to finish.

Main Goals

  • Create a video that catches attention and stands out on the Shopify App Store
  • Create a logo that fits the nature of the app

Behind the Scenes

A look at how the video came together — from footage setup to final edit.
