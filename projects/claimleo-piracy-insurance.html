ClaimLeo - Piracy Insurance

Creating an AI-generated marketing video for a piracy insurance product.

Project Summary

"How do you make a piracy insurance product feel cinematic and urgent — without a film crew?"

ClaimLeo needed a marketing video that communicated the stakes of software piracy and the value of their insurance product. Rather than shooting traditional footage, this project leaned fully into AI-generated video to build a compelling visual narrative.

Using AI video generation tools, I was able to rapidly prototype scenes, iterate on the look and feel, and compose a polished final edit — all without leaving the desk.

Main Goals

  • Create a compelling marketing video for the ClaimLeo piracy insurance product
  • Explore and apply AI video generation in a real production workflow
  • Deliver a polished, branded final edit with logo and motion graphics

Tools Used

Premiere Pro

For composing the video clips and text animation.

Higgsfield: Nano Banana 2 + Seedance 2.0

For generating AI video content used throughout the video.

Figma

For logo refining and appearance.

Adobe Stock

For sourcing the original home-office image used at the beginning of the video.
