Vendidit Website

Front facing website built for conversion and brand perception elevation.

Project Summary

“Customers needed to know who we were and what we offered”

I was given full creative direction on the most recent version of the Vendidit.com website. The first iteration loosely mirrored sites like the marketplace Fastlane. I built the present version under the direction of demand from our CFO, and others from market channels. Thereafter a lighter, design-heavy refresh was initiated and a rework was created in Figma.

The website was designed on Figma and built on Hubspot CMS.

Main Goals

  • Clarity – The information needed to clearly explain in as little time as possible to the customer what Vendidit does and who it's for.
  • Conversion – After seeing our product is the right fit, the value prop, we just had to win them over on trust and action.
  • Quality – Despite the shortcomings of Hubspot CMS, the look and feel of the site represents our brand’s desire to have a feeling impression of quality.
