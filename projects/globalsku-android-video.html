GlobalSKU Android Music Video

Creating a music video to announce GlobalSKU's Android launch.

Project Summary

"As an Android user myself, I've been wanting this release. How do you announce it in a way that gets people excited?"

We needed to make a video for the Android release. I'm an Android user, and have been annoyed with having to not use my phone for Android. We've been experimenting with marketing methods with videos, and I wanted to make a music video.

I had recently watched the Men in Black music video and was inspired, but needed to get something out by Monday.

Main Goals

  • Announce the Android release with an engaging video
  • Experiment with video marketing methods
  • Learn and apply new video editing and 3D animation techniques

Tools Used

Premiere Pro

For editing the footage, keyframe animation, and effects.

Blender

For creating 3D text animations and lighting setups.

The Inspiration

I had recently watched the Men in Black music video and was inspired by it. I wanted to make a music video for the Android release, but needed to get something out by Monday.

I recorded the footage pretty roughly in my room, and after cutting in the right parts and adding some effects, I actually couldn't believe how it was turning out.

Video Editing

I learned a lot about video editing during the process, specifically keyframe animation and how all the effects are based off of values that are affected in the effects and property panel, and how the keyframe affects their status.

Along with using the curves to change the ease settings. Understanding these fundamentals opened up a lot of creative possibilities for timing and pacing the visuals.

3D Animations in Blender

I used Blender to create the 3D animations. I had a lot of room to get creative and learn some new tricks.

The movement and pacing of the words was the most interesting part. If I just stopped the movement of the words, it would be jarring, so I had to add this slow flowing effect after they stop to keep it smooth. So it'd keep flowing nice and slowly after it stops.

Lighting

Getting the lighting right was also interesting. I had to add so many lights to get it to look the way I wanted.

Final Result

The final video came together better than expected. What started as a rough recording in my room became something I'm genuinely proud of.
