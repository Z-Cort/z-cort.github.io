Professional Summary

With three years working in the industry and a lifelong passion for design, I have a sharp eye for what resonates with viewers.

I combine strategy with design to deliver meaningful content that gets results.

I graduated from Texas State University with a Bachelors in Business Management. Unsure of what path to take, I began my career at a loan company. After dealing with poorly designed software and marketing materials, I decided I wanted to be the person doing the designing.

I believed I had what it takes to bring success to my teammates.

So I spent years studying UX and modern design principles with the purpose of solving problems. I accomplished my goals and successfully made a career change to a Product Manager at Vendidit.

Now I'm in charge of our official website, brand design, UI Design, refining new product features, and creating marketing material. My role bridges the gaps between the needs of our marketing department and product development teams.
